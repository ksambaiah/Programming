*nix systems (Unix/Linux) all come with cron, we regularly say cron entry.

What is meant by cron entry?

man page says
cron -- daemon to execute scheduled commands

Listing of existing crontab entries
crontab -l

Editing cron entries
crontab -e

examples of cron entries
21 10 * * *  /path_of_file

21 0-23/4 * * * /path_of_file

First command runs every day at 10.21 hours
Second command runs every four hours at 21 minutes

What are next three stars?

After hour * refers to day valid values are 1-31

After day next is month 

After month day of the week

12 13 3 4 * /path
It runs 3'rd day of 4'th month at 13.12

Where are the entries stored?
/var/spool/cron/username

Apart from system cron entries run from /etc/crontabxxx directories
